    Product Details

    Outdoor billboards mainly stand next to the highway, including highways, urban roads, overpasses and other major roads next to the establishment.

    PVC frontlit banner is the main material to print the advertisement picture on the billboard. With more than 15 years experience manufacturering frontlit banner material, AFLAX DIGITAL SIGNAGE has established skilled researching and production team for frontlit billboard banner.

    AFLAX DIGITAL SIGNAGE frontlit banner for billboard with following advantages:

    • High tearing and breaking strength: with high yarn application, AFLAX DIGITAL SIGNAGE is able to produce stronger billboard banner material.
    • Good picture performance: With professional production experience, AFLAX DIGITAL SIGNAGE frontlit banner material adopt seprate raw material composition for different country in different season, to make the very same good performance.
    • Consistent quality: For orders from same customer, we use the same calendar, same lamination machine, same raw material to make the very same frontlit banner product at the same quality.

    AFLAX DIGITAL SIGNAGE produce and supply frontlit banner for billboard to more than 90 countries all over the world. The consistant quality and competitive price has won good reputation among customers.
