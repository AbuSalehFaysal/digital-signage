    Everything You Need for Digital Signage

    From being able to make easy updates to creating game-day playlists that feature time-sensitive promotions, you can use NoviSign to manage all of your screen content. Pre-create and schedule your playlists, set recurring and expiration dates for content and more! All content caches on media player plus come with the ability to monitor all screens, the status and run proof-of-play reports.

    Digital Signage for Stadiums and Arenas

    Use digital signage for stadiums and arenas in your tickets stands, hallways, concession stands, restaurants, and any other high traffic location. By displaying informative venue specific information, you will add to value your customers experience while gaining full control over what is displayed on every screen.

    • Add live Instagram, Facebook and Twitter feeds
    • Incorporate team games day news and information
    • Advertise team jersey, hat and team merchandise products
    • Add live game updates, scores and team stats

    Why NoviSign?

    NoviSign’s Digital Signage for Stadiums and arenas is a robust and fully scalable solution for managing your sports facilities digital signage. From any computer users can remotely create, edit and update every screen. Setup multi-user accounts with admin and audit control allowing the process of content management easy and efficient.

    • Use any screen, tablet or kiosk
    • Runs on Chrome OS, Android and Windows media players
    • No setup fees or required maintenance contracts
    • Cloud-based and comes with over 20+ drag-and-drop widgets
    • All content caches and play if your network is down
    • Remote monitoring and advanced scheduling
    • Full training and support is included
