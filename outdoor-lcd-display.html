    Commercial grade sun readable HD displays for 24/7 outdoor applications

    Our High Brightness Outdoor Digital Signage is manufactured specifically to perform in brightly lit environments, even in direct sunlight, making it ideal for outdoor applications. With a fully protected IP65 rated enclosure, you can be confident in this screen’s reliability and longevity. Pro Display offers additional extras for this product, including a WiFi / LAN module, enabling remote updating of content and scheduling.

    The commercial grade Outdoor Digital Signage Displays are designed for 24/7 use, offering high contrast, excellent colour reproduction with a backlight producing a high brightness display of 1,500 nits.

    The advantages of this screen are multitude. From its outdoor suitability to its high brightness specification, our Outdoor LCD Digital Signage displays have been designed for effective outdoor use, so you can make sure that your advert, brand message or information is not only being displayed, but displayed effectively.

    This screen is also compatible with Pro Display’s Interactive Touch Foils, which enables an interactive touch functionality, offering a whole range of new and engaging applications for your audience.

    Available as a wall mounted screen from sizes 32″ through to 86″ and a freestanding advertising kiosk in sizes 49″ to 86″, in 16:9 aspect ratio, the Outdoor High Brightness Screen is perfect for most typical digital signage applications.

    Complete with optional wall mount bracket and branding; including coloured fascias, custom graphics and logos, this screen is the ideal fit for any business who wants to shine brighter than the competition.
